Step 2: Connect via SSH

In EC2 Dashboard, select your instance and copy the Public IPv4
Open Terminal and run: 
ssh -i /path/to/your/my-aws-key.pem ubuntu@your-ec2-public-ip
You should now see the Ubuntu welcome message.

If you get a permissions error, you may need to adjust the permissions on your key file with: chmod 400 /path/to/your/my-aws-key.pem
